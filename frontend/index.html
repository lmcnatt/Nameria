Welcome to Nameria

Where Narnia Meets Humblewood

Step into a magical realm where the boundary between beast and humanoid blurs. In Nameria, all creatures are either fully animals blessed with intelligence and purpose, or humanoids who carry the essence of the animal kingdom within them. Here, a society of animal-folk thrives in harmony with the ancient forests, building civilizations that honor both their primal nature and their civilized hearts.

A Primal World

Inspired by the wonder of Narnia and the charm of Humblewood, Nameria is a land where magic flows through every creature and ancient forests hold untold mysteries.
